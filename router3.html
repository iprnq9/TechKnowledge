Wi-Fi Plant Killer Project
Jimmy Monsaine
  • Description

    There have been recent studies that show that plants placed near wireless routers will turn brown and die. I want to perform my own study to analyze the effect that radio waves from a router will have on nearby plants. If Wi-Fi signals are killing plants, how does this effect animals and humans that are frequently nearby a wireless router?
  • What I Need

    • Wireless router
  • Why I Need It

    I have a laptop, but I don't have enough money to buy a wireless router for this experiment. My family and I can budget enough money to buy internet service from the local provider, but I don't want to lease out a router from the local provider because I'm going to take it apart and learn about the internal components. If anyone can donate a spare wireless router I would appreciate it!
