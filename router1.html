Wifi Blocker Project
Rachel Brickston
  • Description

    Many people experience weak Wi-Fi reception on smartphones, tablets, and laptops. Certain materials can actually block a Wi-Fi signal. I want to find out which materials are part of the problem. In this science project, I will do an experiment to find out which materials cause the biggest drop in signal strength from a wireless router.
  • What I Need

    • Wireless router
    • Wifi enabled laptop
  • Why I Need It

    I live in a low income area, and my family is on a limited budget because my father needs surgery. I'm very interested in network connection because everyone is connected to the internet these days. I think I could learn more by actually experimenting with a wireless router and a laptop, but I don't have either.
